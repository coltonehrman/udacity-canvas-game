Frogger Game by Colton Ehrman

How To Play: Use ARROW keys to move player around map.

Objective: Grab the KEY and make it to the top of the map without being hit by a bug to advance to next level...

TIPS: Grabbing GEMS is optional but gives you 1000 points, Getting hit by a bug deducts 500 points from SCORE...

TIPS: You cannot move over ROCKS, but you can grab what is inside of it by running into it...

TIPS: HEARTS will randomly spawn and give you and extra LIFE when picking one up, game starts with 5 LIVES...
